TITLE : THE VOYEURS


RELEASE DATE : September 10, 2021

CAST : Sydney Sweeney,Justice Smith,Ben Hardy,Natasha Liu Bordizzo

AGE RESTRICTION : 16VL

Runtime: 2hr

KA young couple, Pippa and Thomas, move into their first apartment together in Montreal. Pippa, who has spent most of her life in school, is eager to live more adventurously. The two soon realize that their windows look directly into the apartment across the street. A man with a professional photography studio is taking pictures of a woman, and Pippa and Thomas watch as they begin having sex in the kitchen. Thomas continues to watch at first, but stops at Pippa's insistence. They jokingly give their neighbors pseudo names, Margot and Brent.
